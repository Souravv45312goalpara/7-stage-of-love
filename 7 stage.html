7 STAGE OF LOVE

The 7 Stages of Love, rooted in Sufi and Persian traditions, describe a spiritual journey from initial attraction to complete union with the beloved (or the Divine), progressing through phases

Dilkashi (Attraction):

Dilkashi (دلکشی) is an Urdu/Persian word meaning attraction, charm, loveliness, beauty, or something delightful/heart-pleasing, derived from "dil" (heart) and "kash" (drawer/attractor), signifying something that "draws the heart" The initial spark, a magnetic pull, or interest in someone.
⭐ Meaning of Dilkashi
  • Attraction / charm
  • Something that pulls the heart
  • Heart-pleasing beauty
  • Impressive personality

Uns (Infatuation/Attachment):
“Uns” is an Urdu–Arabic word that describes a deep emotional attachment or a feeling of warm closeness with someone or something. It is a positive, comforting emotion. Intense preoccupation, feeling overcome by the person.

Ishq (Love): Deep affection and connection develops, moving beyond infatuation.

Akidat (Trust/Reverence): A deep respect and faith in the partner, sharing vulnerabilities.

Ibadat (Worship): Seeing the divine in the beloved, devotion and cherishing them.

Junoon (Madness/Ecstasy): A passionate, almost uncontrollable state, feeling one cannot live without them.

Maut (Annihilation/Death): The ego dissolves; "me" becomes "we," merging into oneness with the beloved or the divine.
