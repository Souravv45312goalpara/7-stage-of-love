    <h2 style="color: black;">7 STAGE OF LOVE</h2>
    <p1 style="color: black;">The 7 Stages of Love, rooted in Sufi and Persian traditions, describe a spiritual journey from initial attraction to complete union with the beloved (or the Divine), progressing through phases</p1><br>
     <br>
    <p2 ><i style="color:antiquewhite;">Dilkashi (Attraction):</i> <br>
     Dilkashi (دلکشی) is an Urdu/Persian word meaning attraction, charm, loveliness, beauty, or something delightful/heart-pleasing, derived from "dil" (heart) and "kash" (drawer/attractor), signifying something that "draws the heart"
    The initial spark, a magnetic pull, or interest in someone.</p2> <br>
    ⭐ Meaning of Dilkashi
    <ul>
    <li>Attraction / charm</li>
    <li>Something that pulls the heart</li>
    <li>Heart-pleasing beauty</li>
    <li>Impressive personality</li>
    </ul>
    <p>ज़िंदगी की राहों में, मुस्कान की दिलकशी काफी है,
    मुश्किलें कितनी भी हों, उम्मीद फिर भी बाकी है।</p>
    <br>
    <p3><i style="color: antiquewhite;">Uns (Infatuation/Attachment):</i> <br>
    “Uns” is an Urdu–Arabic word that describes a deep emotional attachment or a feeling of warm closeness with someone or something. It is a positive, comforting emotion.
    Intense preoccupation, feeling overcome by the person.</p3> <br>
    ⭐ What “Uns” Means
    <ul>
        <li>A natural sense of affection</li>
        <li>Feeling comfortable and safe with someone</li>
        <li>A gentle emotional bond</li>
    <li>A sense of belonging or connection</li>
        <p>कई बार उन्स उन लोगों से भी हो जाता है,
जो हमारे साथ कम बोलते हैं, पर दिल समझते हैं।</p>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <p4><i style="color: antiquewhite;">Ishq (Love):</i> Love is a deep feeling of care, trust, and respect for someone.
It is not just about emotions — it also includes understanding, patience, and support.
        Deep affection and connection develops, moving beyond infatuation.</p4><br>
        ⭐ What Love Really Means
        <ul>
            <li>Care: You want good things for the person.</li>
            <li>Respect: You accept their thoughts and boundaries.</li>
            <li>Trust: You feel safe and honest with them.</li>
            <li>Support: You help each other grow.</li>
            <li>Understanding: You listen and don’t judge quickly.</li>
        </ul>
    <p>प्यार की असली खूबसूरती,
एक-दूसरे को बदलने में नहीं,
बल्कि समझने में होती है।</p>
    <br>
    <p5><i style="color: antiquewhite;">Akidat (Trust/Reverence):</i>
        Akidat is a clean, respectful feeling.
It is not based on excitement — it is based on faith, honesty, and sincerity.
        A deep respect and faith in the partner, sharing vulnerabilities.</p5><br>
    ⭐ What Akidat really means
    <ul>
        <li>Deep respect</li>
        <li>Strong belief</li>
        <li>Sincere trust</li>
        <li>Heartfelt devotion</li>
        <li>Pure admiration</li>
    </ul>
    <p>आक़ीदत की सबसे बड़ी पहचान यह है,
कि यह ज़ोर नहीं डालती, बस दिल से सम्मान देती है।</p>
    <br>
    <p6><i style="color: antiquewhite;">Ibadat (Worship):</i> 
        Ibadat is not only about rituals —
it is about the intention behind your actions.
        Seeing the divine in the beloved, devotion and cherishing them.</p6><br>
    ⭐ What Ibadat Really Means
    <ul>
        <li>A clean heart</li>
        <li>Honest behavior</li>
        <li>Patience</li>
        <li>Gratitude</li>
        <li>Kindness to others</li>
    </ul>
    <p>सच्ची इबादत वह है,
जो दिल को नम्र बना दे और ज़ुबान को साफ़।</p>
    <br>
    <p7><i style="color: antiquewhite;">Junoon (Madness/Ecstasy):</i> “Junoon” is an Urdu–Arabic word that describes a strong, unstoppable inner passion.
It is not harmful madness — it is the fire inside someone that pushes them to work hard, focus deeply, and chase a goal with full energy.
        A passionate, almost uncontrollable state, feeling one cannot live without them.</p7><br>
    ⭐ What Junoon Really Means
    <ul>
        <li>Strong inner motivation</li>
        <li>A feeling that pushes you beyond normal limits</li>
        <li>Deep excitement and focus</li>
        <li>A powerful urge to achieve something</li>
    </ul>
    <p>
        “जुनून इस बात का रखो कि तुम अपनी मंज़िल पा लो,
किसी इंसान को नहीं, अपनी क़ाबिलियत को जीत लो।”
    </p>
    <br>
    <p8><i style="color: antiquewhite;">Maut (Annihilation/Death):</i> the death of distance, the death of fears, the death of the old you—so that a new version, shaped by love, can rise. The ego dissolves; "me" becomes "we," merging into oneness with the beloved or the divine. </p8><br>
    <p>True love is never end befor death , if the love is end befor deth then it isnot love </p>
